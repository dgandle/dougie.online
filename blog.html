Found on the internet: MiniDV Moments

I’ve digging around on the internet recently and found this YouTube channel called “MiniDV Moments.” Each video is like a little tableau, often just trees or a sunset, sometimes more personal moments. I especially love the ambient, gentle hum of the camera’s microphone present in some of the quieter clips. The creator says the bulk of the videos were filmed between 2006-2009. Really cool and beautiful stuff.
